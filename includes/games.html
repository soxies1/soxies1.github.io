Escape dimension

Made in 48Hrs for BC Game Jam 2017, with the given theme of "Dimensions", Escape Dimension combines escape room game play with a Portal feel. The objective: to escape the experimentation facility before the clock hits 12. To overcome obstacles, teleport between dimensions. Made in Unity3D.

Raft It

Made in 48Hrs for Global Game Jam 2017, with the given theme of "Waves", collect more bouyent objects than your opponent, while avoiding being swept by the encroaching sea. Fill your opponents crate with heavy objects to subtract their score. Don't be afraid to be aggressive and try to tackle your opponent into the sea. Made in Unity2D.
